We believe privacy should be simple. This policy explains in plain language what data we collect, why we collect it, and what you can do about it. We do not sell your personal data. Ever.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Supacms platform, including visitors to our website and registered account holders. It explains how Supacms ("we", "us", "our") collects, processes, stores, and protects your personal information.

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our Service.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary to provide and improve the Service. Here's a breakdown of the categories:

Account Information
Name, Email Address, Company Name
Provided when you register for an account.
Billing Information
Payment method details (processed by our Merchant of Record)
We do not store your full card number on our servers. Payment data is handled securely by our third-party payment processor.
Usage & Device Data
Screen connection logs, playlist activity, login timestamps, IP addresses
Collected automatically to ensure platform reliability and detect anomalies.
User Content
Images, videos, text, and HTML files you upload
Stored securely on our cloud infrastructure solely to deliver the Service to your screens.
Communications
Support emails, feedback messages
Kept to resolve your enquiry and improve our support experience.

3. How We Collect Data

We collect data through the following means:

  • Directly from you — when you register, subscribe, upload content, or contact us
  • Automatically — via server logs, device pairing events, and usage analytics
  • From third parties — our payment processor shares transaction confirmation data with us
  • Cookies and local storage — to maintain your session and preferences (see Section 6)

4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To operate, maintain, and deliver the Supacms platform
  • Account management: To create and manage your account, authenticate logins, and process plan upgrades
  • Billing & payments: To process subscription fees, issue invoices, and handle refund requests
  • Customer support: To respond to your questions, troubleshoot issues, and resolve disputes
  • Service improvement: To analyse usage patterns and improve platform performance and features
  • Security: To detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect the platform and its users
  • Legal compliance: To meet applicable legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements
  • Communications: To send important service updates, security alerts, and (where you've opted in) product news

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build advertising profiles or sell your data to ad networks, data brokers, or any third parties.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We share data with trusted third-party vendors who help us run the platform (e.g., cloud hosting via Supabase/AWS, payment processing). These vendors are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.
  • Legal obligations: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Protection of rights: We may disclose data to enforce our Terms of Service, protect the safety and security of our users, or investigate fraud.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you via email and a prominent notice on the platform prior to any such transfer.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use a minimal number of cookies strictly necessary to operate the Service. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking.

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in while using the dashboard. These are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your dashboard settings and UI preferences.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage data to understand how the platform is used so we can improve it (e.g., page load performance, feature usage frequency).

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling session cookies will prevent you from logging in to the Service.

7. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase, built on Amazon Web Services (AWS). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive account data
  • Access controls limiting which personnel can access customer data
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Automated backups to prevent data loss

While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your account
  • After cancellation: We retain account data for 90 days post-cancellation to allow for reactivation and dispute resolution, after which it is permanently deleted
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years to comply with applicable accounting and tax laws
  • Support communications: Retained for 2 years after the issue was resolved

You may request earlier deletion of your data at any time (see Section 9 — Your Rights).

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request corrections to any inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at nabeelsaleemshaikh@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at individuals under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at nabeelsaleemshaikh@gmail.com and we will take prompt action to delete that information.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
